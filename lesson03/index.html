Site Name

The name of the site is "Avellaneda Chamber of Commerce".

Site Purpose

Our purpose is for business owners in the city of Avellaneda to develop strong connections at the local level and to bring the business community together. This association exists as an open space for entrepreneurs, producers and service providers to work as a team in order to defend and develop our industries. Businesses interested in joining the group are invited to register and apply on our website.

Site Domain

The site's domain name will be avlcomm.org, which can be purchased through Google Domains for $12/year.
