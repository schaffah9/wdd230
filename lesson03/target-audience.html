Target Audience Description

The target audience of the website includes entrepreneurs, business owners, professional and general public interested in growing their business and develop strong connections within the community.

Personas

  • Persona 1: Young male entrepreneur, single, in his early 30s, looking to connect with potential investors in his e-commerce business. He has a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration. He's highly proficient in computer skills because his laptop is his main work tool.
  • Persona 2: Experienced businessman, close to retirement age, with strong ties in the community. Married, 2 children and 3 grandkids. He manages to do some simple tasks in his old computer, like sending emails, but struggles with more complex tasks and usually asks someone else for help. His education comprises a highschool degree and some college courses. He's hoping to team up with other companies in order to expand his operation in the area.

Scenarios

  1. How can I get in contact with potential investors for my growing business?
  2. What benefits does the local government has to offer to small and medium-sized enterprises?
  3. What paperwork is required to register a company in Avellaneda?
  4. Where can I find business and commercial lawyers and accountants with great references?
  5. What are some local stories of successfully growing businesses?
